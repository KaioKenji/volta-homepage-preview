Boundaries that prevent duplicate or unsafe messages

  • Provider ownershipStripe owns payment/KYC/rail notices; Supabase is first choice for A1/A2 Auth messages. Do not add a duplicate TSP send until a recipient/action gap is proven.
  • Retry ownershipOnly bounded transient or uncertain failures may retry with the same idempotency key. A confirmed send, invalid payload, or completed send is not retried.
  • Scope ownershipKeep the pilot hotel-scoped. A future group summary needs explicit group authorization; do not render a group rollup as a pilot capability.
